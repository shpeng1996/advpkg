# MediaTek CEO Reaffirms TSMC as Key Long-Term Partner; Google v8e TPU → Intel EMIB Confirmed

## 核心主張 / Key Claims

1. Samsung Chairman Jay Y. Lee discreetly visited Taiwan week of 2026-05-22, reportedly seeking to lure MediaTek foundry orders from TSMC via a memory+foundry incentive bundle
2. MediaTek CEO Rick Tsai (shareholder meeting 2026-05-29) reaffirmed TSMC as "most important and long-term" partner across all nodes from 12nm to 1.4nm
3. **MediaTek confirmed Intel EMIB packaging for Google v8e (inference TPU)**; training TPUs retained at TSMC CoWoS
4. Samsung's dual incentive: priority Dimensity memory supply in exchange for foundry order shift — similar to earlier Qualcomm approach
5. Samsung targeting AMD and MediaTek as next major 2nm foundry wins, after securing Tesla AI6

## 關鍵數據 / Key Data Points

| 項目 | 數據 |
|------|------|
| MediaTek–TSMC nodes | 12nm, 7nm, 6nm, 5nm, 2nm, 1.4nm |
| MediaTek N2P SoC tape-out | Sep 2025; volume production late 2026 |
| Google v8e TPU packaging | Intel EMIB (confirmed per Commercial Times) |
| Google training TPU | TSMC CoWoS (retained) |
| Samsung Tesla AI6 foundry | Confirmed at Samsung |
| MediaTek CPO investment | 400G SerDes + CPO (continued) |

## 新增知識 / New Knowledge Added

- **Intel EMIB 客戶具體化**：Google v8e 採用 Intel EMIB 封裝，由 MediaTek 代為設計，是迄今 wiki 記錄最具體的 EMIB 客戶落地案例（先前只知道 Google 在評估 EMIB）
- **Samsung 記憶體+代工捆綁策略首次披露**：Jay Y. Lee 親赴台灣的目的鎖定 MediaTek，以 Dimensity 記憶體優先供應換代工訂單，屬新情報
- **MediaTek 1.4nm 節點合作確認**：Rick Tsai 點名 1.4nm 作為未來合作範圍，這是 wiki 首次記錄 MediaTek 在 A14 節點的官方聲明

## 矛盾或修正 / Contradictions / Corrections
