# HBM4 Strategies Diverge: Samsung Chases 80% Yield, SK Hynix Trims 30% / TrendForce 2026-04-15

## 核心主張 / Key Claims

- **Samsung** HBM4 1c DRAM yields remain **below 60%** as of April 2026; target is 80% by H2 2026 — the conventional mass-production maturity threshold
- **SK Hynix** is cutting HBM4 shipments by **20–30%** from original target due to NVIDIA Rubin platform delays; volumes redirected to HBM3E and server DRAM (LPDDR)
- **NVIDIA Rubin** platform share slips from **29% → 22%** of high-end GPU shipments in 2026; **Blackwell** (HBM3E-based) climbs from **61% → 71%**
- Samsung has raised **HBM4 logic die prices by 40–50%** since early 2026, supported by 4nm process running at high utilization
- **Micron**: non-HBM margins (DDR5) currently **higher than HBM** margins; projects ~81% gross margin 3QFY26

## 新增知識 / New Knowledge Added

1. **Samsung HBM4 yield crisis量化**：首次以具體數字（<60% vs 80% 目標）記錄三星 HBM4 的良率困境；背後原因包含 EUV 層數較多（成本高）和疊層封裝額外製程（即使晶片良率提升，封裝段仍可能下降）
2. **SK Hynix HBM4 下調的根本原因**：Rubin 平台延遲（CX8→CX9 網路互連切換、液冷需求升級、HBM4 11 Gbps 目標未達），導致供應商主動下調而非市場需求疲軟
3. **Micron 獲利結構轉折**：DDR5 等非 HBM 產品毛利率已超越 HBM，顛覆先前「HBM 最有利潤」的市場共識；對評估 HBM 投資優先順序具有重要指標意義
4. **Blackwell vs Rubin 市場份額移動**：Rubin 延遲直接嘉惠 Blackwell/HBM3E 出貨量

## 矛盾或修正 / Contradictions / Corrections

- 與 wiki/technologies/hbm4.md 記錄的「HBM4 2026 年強勁需求」部分矛盾：本文指出 Rubin 延遲導致 HBM4 短期需求下修；但長期趨勢（H2 2026 後）仍看好。需在 hbm4.md 補充 Rubin 延遲的短期影響。
- 與 wiki/entities/sk-hynix.md 記錄的「M15X 提前投產」需補充說明：產能提升後反而因 Rubin 延遲而下調出貨量，形成「產能充裕但需求暫緩」的局面。
