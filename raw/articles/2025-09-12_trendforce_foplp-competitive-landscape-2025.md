# FOPLP Trial Yields Reportedly Reach 90% at Powertech as TSMC Advances CoPoS Development

**Source**: TrendForce — published 2025-09-12

## Key Facts

### Powertech FOPLP Milestone
- **Powertech Technology** (OSAT) trial FOPLP yields reportedly reaching **~90%** as of September 2025
- Panel size: **515 × 510 mm** (Powertech standard)
- Significant yield improvement indicates FOPLP moving from R&D toward pilot production

### Competitive Panel-Level Packaging Landscape (2025)

| Player | Role | Panel Size | Status (2025) |
|--------|------|-----------|----------------|
| TSMC (CoPoS) | Foundry | 310 × 310 mm | Pilot line 2026; MP 2028–29 |
| Samsung | IDM | TBD (glass substrate) | Competitive development |
| ASE Group | OSAT | 600 × 600 mm | Expanding capacity |
| Powertech | OSAT | 515 × 510 mm | 90% trial yield reached |
| Innolux | Display → Pkg | 700 × 700 mm | Large-format panel leverage |

### TSMC CoPoS Specifics
- TSMC is using **glass** as the panel material (vs. competitors using other materials)
- Through-Glass Via (TGV) under development alongside FOPLP
- Mini line expected set up by **2026**, gradual ramp in **2027**
- Limited trial production in Taoyuan anticipated as early as **2027**
- Full CoPoS production at Chiayi AP7: **2028–2029**

### Samsung FOPLP Approach
- Samsung and TSMC in "close combat on FOPLP with different panel materials"
- Samsung exploring non-glass substrate materials for differentiation

### Resonac Ecosystem (Sept 2025)
- **Resonac** teaming up with **Applied Materials**, **TEL**, and chip giants on **panel-level interposers**
- Collaboration signals growing materials/equipment ecosystem for panel packaging

### InFO Context
- TSMC InFO (Integrated Fan-Out) remains in wafer-level format (FOWLP), used since iPhone 7 (2016)
- InFO capacity at AP5 (Taichung) and AP6 (Zhunan) serving mobile/networking
- FOPLP is the panel-scale evolution of InFO-class fan-out packaging
- Key benefit of panel over wafer: **more dies per run**, lower cost per package
