Billerica, USA, May 12, 2026 – ASMPT presented advanced packaging solutions for AI and HPC at Booth 100 during the IEEE 76th ECTC 2026, Orlando, Florida, May 26–29.

## FIREBIRD Series — Thermocompression Bonding for Advanced HBM Integration

**Platform features**:
- Active Oxide Removal (AOR) technology: residue-free fluxless bonding, eliminates flux-related cleaning steps
- Proven for 16-layer HBM stacks
- 2.0 µm placement accuracy for fine-pitch, high-I/O-density applications
- >500 TCB systems deployed in mass production worldwide

**Key capability**: Supports advanced AI packaging including multi-layer HBM stacks, combining scalability, throughput, and process stability.

## LITHOBOLT™ G2 — Die-to-Wafer (D2W) Bonding for Next-Generation AI

**Platform features**:
- Engineered for 3D integration and high-density Cu-Cu interconnects
- Contamination-controlled environment
- Void-free bonding interfaces
- Tight overlay accuracy
- Ultra-fine-pitch integration for >10,000 interconnects/mm²
- Addresses logic-memory integration and chiplet architectures

## Context
ASMPT had Q1 2026 orders of 27M+, +71.6% YoY, driven by HBM4 16H TCB demand and CPO (5× growth). The FIREBIRD and LITHOBOLT platforms are ASMPT's primary products for the HBM and chiplet bonding markets.

Jean Marc Peallat, Regional Head, ASMPT Semiconductor Solutions Americas:
"Advanced packaging is becoming a critical enabling technology for the AI era. At ECTC 2026, ASMPT will demonstrate how our technologies help manufacturers scale increasingly complex AI and HPC architectures for high-volume production."
