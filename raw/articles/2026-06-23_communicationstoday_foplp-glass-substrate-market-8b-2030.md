# FOPLP, glass substrate market to surge past $8B by 2030

Reporting on Counterpoint Research's "2026 FOPLP and Glass Substrate Report."

## Key Claims

1. Combined global FOPLP + Glass Substrate Packaging (GSP) market forecast to grow from ~$650M (2024) to >$8.1B by 2030.
2. AI/HPC applications will be the largest growth driver, reaching 45.6% of total FOPLP market revenue by 2030.
3. Glass substrates are gaining attention as an alternative to organic substrates due to better interconnect density, dimensional stability, and warpage control for chiplet-based/large AI processor architectures.
4. TSMC (CoPoS), Intel, Samsung Electro-Mechanics, ASE, and PTI are named as investing in glass substrate / panel-level packaging capacity.
5. East Asia (Taiwan, Japan, Mainland China) projected to hold 84.8% of global panel-level packaging capacity by 2030; Japan singled out for particularly strong capacity growth in glass substrate production.
6. Remaining technical hurdles: panel size standardization, Through-Glass Via (TGV) process consistency, manufacturing scalability.
