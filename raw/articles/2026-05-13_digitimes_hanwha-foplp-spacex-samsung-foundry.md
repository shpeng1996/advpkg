# Hanwha Semitech to supply FO-PLP equipment for SpaceX-linked chip production

*Published: May 13, 2026 — DIGITIMES Asia (Paywalled — summary only)*

South Korean semiconductor equipment maker **Hanwha Semitech** is reportedly preparing to supply **fan-out panel-level packaging (FO-PLP) equipment** for advanced chip packaging in the second half of 2026. The systems are expected to be used in the mass production of networking chips for **SpaceX**.

**Key points (from article metadata and related stories):**
- Hanwha Semitech: South Korean equipment maker specializing in TCB (Thermo-Compression Bonding) and FO-PLP systems
- Has existing relationships with SK Hynix (HBM production equipment) and has been courting Foxconn and ASE for OSAT business
- Was involved in a patent dispute with Hanmi Semiconductor over TCB technology
- FO-PLP equipment delivery timing: 2H 2026
- End customer: SpaceX (networking chips for Starlink or related satellite infrastructure)

**Significance:**
- First reported instance of FO-PLP equipment supply specifically linked to SpaceX chip production
- Confirms space/satellite market as a new demand vector for advanced panel-level packaging
- Hanwha Semitech competes with Hanmi Semiconductor and ASMPT in TCB/FO-PLP equipment space
