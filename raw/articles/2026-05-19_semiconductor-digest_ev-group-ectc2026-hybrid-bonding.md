EV Group (EVG) announced it will showcase its latest solutions for heterogeneous integration and advanced packaging at the 2026 IEEE Electronic Components and Technology Conference (ECTC), taking place May 26-29 in Orlando, Fla.

Featured systems:
- GEMINI FB production wafer bonding systems (de facto W2W hybrid bonding standard)
- EVG 40 D2W die-to-wafer overlay metrology system
- IR LayerRelease layer transfer technology platform
- LITHOSCALE XT maskless exposure system (5× throughput vs. previous industry benchmark)

EVG's process solutions enable manufacturing advances in: HBM, CFETs, 3D/chiplet integration, and advanced packaging applications.

## Key Technical Notes

**GEMINI FB:** Industry standard for W2W hybrid and fusion bonding; exceptional alignment and overlay accuracy in high-productivity platform.

**EVG40 D2W:** In-line overlay metrology for D2W bonding; supports real-time feedback loops to maximize die placement accuracy.

**IR LayerRelease:** IR laser passes through silicon (transparent at IR wavelength); enables laser-initiated release of ultra-thin films/layers from silicon carriers with nm precision; eliminates glass substrates for certain advanced packaging flows; enables 3D sequential integration with ultra-thin layers.

**LITHOSCALE XT:** 5× throughput increase over previous benchmark; enables high-resolution patterning for FOWLP, fine-pitch probe cards, advanced image sensors, and die traceability.
