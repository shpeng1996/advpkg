## Summary

IEEE Electronics Packaging Society (EPS) published a summary (2026-03) of the ECTC 2025 Special Session on Hybrid Bonding, covering the state of Cu-Cu direct bonding technology for 3D IC integration.

### ECTC 2025 Conference Overview
- **75th annual IEEE ECTC**, held May 27–30, 2025 at Gaylord Texan Resort & Convention Center
- ~400 technical papers across 36 oral and 5 interactive sessions; 12 special sessions
- Topics: heterogeneous integration, photonics, components, materials, assembly, reliability, modeling, interconnect design, 2.5D/3D/3.5D integration, direct/hybrid bonding, device/system packaging, wafer-level packaging

### Hybrid Bonding Integration Scenarios (from Special Session)
Three distinct scenarios with unique maturity levels:
1. **Wafer-to-Wafer (W2W)**: Already in production (e.g., memory stacking); highest maturity
2. **Die-to-Wafer (D2W)**: Progressing rapidly; enables logic-memory and logic-logic stacking
3. **Die-to-Die (D2D)**: Emerging; used in chiplet architectures and repair functions

### Key Technical Findings
- Hybrid bonding enables **sub-10 µm bond pitch** (vs. micro-bumps at ~40–50 µm)
- HCB (Hybrid Cu Bonding) achieves sub-10 µm pitch with improved electrical performance; no power/signal penalty
- Target: **1M interconnects/mm²** density over next few years
- Thermal stress characterization: new metrology methods for visualizing hybrid bonding induced thermal stress

### Key Process Challenges
- **Surface defectivity** after CMP (Chemical Mechanical Polishing)
- Post-CMP copper and dielectric **surface profile** control
- Pre-bonding wafer and die **warpage control**
- Clean singulation (plasma dicing)
- **Bonding alignment** accuracy at single-micron scales
- Defect inspection becomes harder as pitch shrinks into single microns

### Industry Participants at ECTC 2025
- EV Group: Hybrid bonding, maskless lithography, layer transfer solutions highlighted
- Intel Foundry: Chiplet-based design and heterogeneous integration innovations
- Chiplet fault isolation and failure analysis special session

### Interconnect Density Roadmap
- Current production: ~10 µm pitch (W2W)
- Near-term: sub-5 µm pitch (D2W)
- Target: 1 µm and below (hybrid bonding for 3D chiplet stacking)
