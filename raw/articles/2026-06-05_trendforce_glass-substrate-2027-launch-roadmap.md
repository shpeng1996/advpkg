# Glass Substrates Eye 2027 Launch, Scale Toward 2030 as CoWoS Costs Rise and Hyperscaler Demand Grows

**Published**: 2026-06-05 | **Source**: TrendForce (citing Sisa Journal, Economic Daily News, Forbes, The Elec)

---

## Timeline

Per Sisa Journal industry sources:
- **2027**: Early commercialization begins
- **2029**: Ramp-up phase
- **2030**: Full-scale mass production

Most likely early end-customers: **NVIDIA** and **Google** (AI accelerator leaders)

## TSMC C.C. Wei Confirms CoPoS Roadmap

At TSMC's **June 4, 2026 shareholder meeting**, Chairman C.C. Wei confirmed:
- TSMC has already built a **CoPoS pilot production line**
- Meaningful ramp-up expected in **about 2–3 years** (i.e., 2028–2029)

## CoWoS Cost as Catalyst

The **ASP of a CoWoS wafer** is ~**$10,000** (comparable to 7nm-class advanced process nodes, per April Commercial Times report), driving demand for glass substrate alternatives.

Glass substrates offer:
- Better heat and warpage resistance
- Higher density connectivity for GPUs and HBM at larger scale

## Company Roadmaps

### SKC / Absolics (Most aggressive investor)
- **KRW 1.2 trillion rights offering** completed
- Plans to inject an additional **KRW 589.6 billion** into Absolics
- Absolics launched new "**non-embedding**" glass substrate project for a U.S. telecommunications chip company
- Performance vs. conventional organic interposers: **-25% thickness**, **>30% power efficiency improvement**
- Target: world's first commercial production **by end-2026**

### Samsung Electro-Mechanics
- **Pilot line at Sejong plant** (Chungnam)
- Mass production target: **2H 2027**
- Currently conducting quality evaluations with **Broadcom** and other major hyperscalers

### LG Innotek
- **Pilot line at Gumi plant**
- Partnered with **UTI** on R&D to improve glass substrate mechanical strength
- UTI is known for ultra-thin glass (UTG) in Samsung foldable phones

### Intel
- Rio Rancho fab producing silicon photonics for external customers
- First **CPO-integrated glass substrate prototypes** showcased
- Commercialization target: **~2030**
