TSMC today announced consolidated revenue of NT$1,270.38 billion, net income of NT$706.56 billion, and diluted earnings per share of NT$27.25 (US$4.31 per ADR unit) for the second quarter ended June 30, 2026.

This marks TSMC's highest-ever quarterly profit and its 10th consecutive quarter of earnings growth. Strong demand for its advanced 3nm and 2nm processes, along with CoWoS advanced packaging, are the key growth engines fueling the company's AI-driven momentum.

Year-over-year, second quarter revenue increased 36.0%, while net income and diluted EPS both increased 77.4%. Compared to first quarter 2026, second quarter results represented a 12.0% increase in revenue and a 23.4% increase in net income.

In US dollars, second quarter revenue was $40.20 billion, which increased 33.7% year-over-year and increased 12.0% from the previous quarter.

Gross margin for the quarter was 67.7%, operating margin was 60.3%, and net profit margin was 55.6%. TSMC's gross margin already surged to a record 66.2% in 1Q26, then climbed even higher in 2Q26 to set another all-time high, surpassing the company's 65.5%–67.5% guidance range.

## Node Mix (2Q26 wafer revenue)
- 2nm: 3%
- 3nm: 30%
- 5nm: 33%
- 7nm: 11%
- Advanced technologies (7nm and more advanced): 77% of total wafer revenue

(Source: TSMC, Reuters)
