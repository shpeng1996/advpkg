# Chip Industry Week In Review #146

**Published:** July 10, 2026 | **Source:** Semiconductor Engineering

## Key Highlights

### Micron
- Raised planned U.S. investment to **more than $250B through 2035**, an incremental $50B above the $200B announced in June 2025, with goal of producing **40% of DRAM in the U.S.**
- New investments of **$3B for U.S. IC supply-chain**, including $500M in financing for **GlobalWafers'** 300mm raw silicon wafer facility in Sherman, Texas, plus 10-year wafer supply agreement
- Entered long-term strategic customer agreement with **Ford** for memory and storage
- Started construction on $9.3B expansion of Hiroshima, Japan advanced memory facility

### Large Deals
- **Apple** announced $30B+ multiyear commitment with **Broadcom** to design and produce custom silicon components and wireless connectivity technologies. Broadcom undertakes $1.5B expansion of Fort Collins, Colorado manufacturing facilities for RF/wireless.
- **Solstice Advanced Materials** agreed to acquire **Element Solutions** for $14.5B (electronics chemicals portfolio)
- **SEALSQ** and **GlobalFoundries** will co-develop secure semiconductor platforms (PQC, cryogenic CMOS)
- **Onsemi** plans to divest Tarlac, Philippines site to Greatek Electronics and Mountain Top, Pennsylvania to Silex Microsystems
- **Sumitomo Chemical's** subsidiary teaming up with **Samsung Electro-Mechanics** on glass core substrates for advanced packaging

### Advanced Packaging
- iMAPS CHIPCon conference in Santa Clara: Amkor SVP David McCann and ASE VP of R&D CP Hung discussed future challenges and directions for advanced packaging
- Samsung (Q2 guidance), TSMC (June sales) and UMC (June revenue) financials released

### Semiconductors/AI
- **SIA/WSTS** reports global semiconductor sales hit record **~$121B in May 2026**, up over 9% from April and over **104% from May 2025**
- **Meta** AI in-house accelerator moving toward manufacturing; goal of doubling compute capacity and reducing Nvidia reliance; reports September production target
- **TetraMem** and **SK hynix** demonstrated analog in-memory computing (resistive memory/IMC) collaboration
- **Flex** and **Cerebras** expanded U.S. production of AI supercomputers (wafer-scale), 7X production ramp through 2026
- **QuantumDiamonds** received $104M for quantum-based semiconductor inspection
- **SambaNova** raised $1B for on-prem agentic inference chips (dataflow architecture)

### Legal
- U.S. ITC reaffirmed patent finding that **Innoscience** infringed Infineon patent (GaN technology); import/sales ban
- **Wolfspeed** filed patent-infringement suit against **Navitas** (GaN-based FETs)

### Workforce
- NNME/SEMI/NSF/McKinsey: U.S. semiconductor industry needs **189K additional workers by 2030**, 74% in manufacturing; 75% of employers had difficulty hiring engineers

### Global
- EU Chips JU opened new application round for advanced research, power electronics, photonics
- Oratomic raised $300M for fault-tolerant quantum computer based on reconfigurable atomic arrays
