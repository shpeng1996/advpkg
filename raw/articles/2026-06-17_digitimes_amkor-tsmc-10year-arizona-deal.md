TSMC and Amkor Technology have signed a 10-year agreement to jointly expand advanced semiconductor packaging capacity in Arizona, a move intended to bring a more complete chip supply chain onto American soil. This follows Amkor's earlier announcement (2026-05-22) of an additional 67-acre expansion of its Arizona packaging campus. Full deal terms and capacity figures are behind DIGITIMES' subscription paywall.
