# Coherent Demonstrates Multiple Technologies for Co-packaged Optics (CPO) at OFC 2026

SAXONBURG, PA, March 17, 2026 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) — Coherent Corp. (NYSE: COHR)

## Key Claims & Data

- Coherent demonstrated multiple CPO architectures at OFC 2026 (Los Angeles), spanning its vertical photonics stack:
  - 6.4T (32×200G) socketed CPO based on silicon photonics, paired with an External Laser Source (ELS) module powered by Coherent's own high-power InP CW lasers.
  - Multimode socketed CPO built with Coherent's high-speed VCSELs.
  - InP modulator on silicon operating at 400G per lane (InP modulator array), illustrating a pathway to higher lane speeds.
- Quote: Dr. Lee Xu (EVP Datacenter, Coherent) — "CPO will be explored in various scale-out and scale-up scenarios by our customers."
- Positions Coherent as a multi-architecture CPO supplier (InP lasers, silicon photonics, VCSELs, advanced packaging) rather than committed to a single optical approach.
