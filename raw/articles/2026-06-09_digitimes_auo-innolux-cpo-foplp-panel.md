# Analysis: Taiwan's two major panel makers enter semiconductor packaging; CPO and FOPLP become key

DIGITIMES analysis, Apr 27/29, 2026 (paywalled — only summary/lede accessible)

## Key claims and data points (from available summary)

- Optical-communication technology is shifting from traditional pluggable optics toward co-packaged optics (CPO) architectures.
- Advanced packaging is extending from wafer-level to panel-level, putting fan-out panel-level packaging (FOPLP) in the spotlight.
- Taiwan's two major display-panel makers — AUO and Innolux — are actively entering the semiconductor-packaging field (CPO and FOPLP) in search of new growth momentum, leveraging their panel manufacturing base.
