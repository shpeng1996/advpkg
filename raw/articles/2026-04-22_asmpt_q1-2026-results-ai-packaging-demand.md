## Advanced Packaging Segment Highlights

### Thermo-Compression Bonding (TCB)
- **Logic C2S (Chip-to-Substrate):** Sizeable shipments and repeat orders in Q1; leadership solidified
- **Logic C2W (Chip-to-Wafer):** 4 tools booked for ultrafine-pitch TCB with proprietary plasma AOR technology from a leading advanced logic customer
- **Memory/HBM4:** Key memory player adopting flux-based TCB tool for sampling and qualifying ASMPT's AOR fluxless process for **HBM4 16-High (16H)**

### Photonics & Co-Packaged Optics (CPO)
- 5× YoY revenue growth (Photonics division)
- Bulk orders for 1.6T transceiver solutions from key optics suppliers in data centre networking
- Deepened engagements with multiple leading global CPO players

### Flip Chip (FC) High-Precision Die-Bonding
- Strong bookings growth from 2.5D packaging for larger AI package sizes
- FC solutions gaining traction for **embedded bridge die bonding** (CoW and CoP applications)
- Expanding into **Panel-Level Fan-Out (PLFO)** for RF and power devices

## Context: NEXX Divestiture
- ASMPT classified NEXX (large-area advanced packaging deposition equipment) as a Discontinued Operation in Q4 2025
- As of Q1 2026 results announcement date (April 22), no binding agreement yet signed — deal with Applied Materials announced May 3, 2026 ($120M cash)
- Q1 2026 figures reflect continuing operations (NEXX excluded); discontinued operation net loss: HK$72.6M

## AI Demand Structural Thesis
ASMPT articulates a clear structural thesis: AI architectures demand heterogeneous integration, tighter interconnect pitch, higher bandwidth, and power efficiency — all driving demand for:
- TCB and Hybrid Bonding (HB) for logic-to-memory interconnects
- High-precision Flip Chip and Mass Reflow for cost-efficient scaling
- Photonics & CPO as connectivity constraints emerge
- Mainstream SEMI/SMT for AI server/infrastructure buildouts
