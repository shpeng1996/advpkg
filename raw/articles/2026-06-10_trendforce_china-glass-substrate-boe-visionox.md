# China Reportedly Narrows Glass Substrate Gap with South Korea as BOE and Visionox Advance

**Published**: 2026-06-10 | **Source**: TrendForce (citing ETNews)

---

## China's Glass Substrate Progress

More than **10 Chinese companies** are now active in the glass substrate market. A representative from a partner company working with a Chinese glass substrate producer stated: **"China has nearly caught up with South Korea in TGV (through-glass via) and etching technologies."**

ETNews expects demand for South Korean products to remain strong during the market's initial growth phase, but China's market share will gradually expand over time.

## BOE's Aggressive Entry

**BOE** was among the first to officially enter the semiconductor glass substrate market:
- Established a **pilot production line within ~2 years** of announcing market entry
- **May 20, 2026**: Signed an MOU with **Corning** (leading display glass supplier)
  - Collaboration scope: glass-core packaging substrates, foldable glass, perovskite glass substrates, optical interconnect applications

## Visionox

**Visionox** (Chinese display manufacturer) is advancing glass substrate efforts:
- Has been building a **materials, components, and equipment supply chain** since 2025
- Continuing to invest in equipment for future glass substrate production

## Technical Achievements

**WG Tech** (Chinese company):
- Achieved TGV process with **aspect ratio of 100:1** and minimum via diameter of **5 micrometers**
- Already delivered **1.6T optical module glass substrates** to customers for sampling

**DR Laser**:
- TGV laser microvia equipment for semiconductor chip packaging
- Has **completed shipments of panel-level glass substrate through-via equipment**

## Global Leader Context

- **Intel**: First CPO-enabled glass substrate prototypes unveiled in May; commercialization targeted by 2030
- **SKC/Absolics**: Could begin world's **first commercial glass substrate production by year-end 2026**
- **Samsung Electro-Mechanics**: Pilot line in Sejong; mass production targeted **after 2027**
- **TSMC**: 310×310mm CoPoS (Chip-on-Panel-on-Substrate) platform, where glass is used as the interposer
