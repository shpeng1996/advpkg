# Onto Innovation's Dragonfly G5 System Qualified for Applications in 2.5D AI Packaging

WILMINGTON, Mass., April 16, 2026 (BUSINESS WIRE)

## Key Claims & Data

- Onto Innovation (NYSE: ONTO) qualified its Dragonfly G5 platform for new and existing 2.5D advanced packaging applications; initial shipments expected June 2026.
- Dragonfly G5 uses new proprietary optics, illumination, and AI-enhanced algorithms improving visibility/throughput and reducing cost of ownership; qualified across "several process layers used for fan-out packaging."
- Industry analysts cited: AI-device demand enabled by packaging advances expected to grow ~30% annually over the next two years, requiring greater process-control sensitivity than prior generations.
- Dragonfly platform shipments (overall) expected to grow >50% in 2026 vs 2025.
- Financial context: Q1 2026 preliminary revenue $292M (beat guidance of $275-285M); Q2 2026 guidance raised to $320-330M (+8% over prior outlook).
- Quotes: Ido Dolev (EVP Product Solutions), Mike Plisinski (CEO).
