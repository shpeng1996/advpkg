## 摘錄 / Excerpt

Naura Technology Group has shipped its first 600mm × 600mm panel-level packaging descum tool, marking a key step in the Chinese semiconductor equipment maker's move from wafer-level packaging into panel-level packaging.

Source: *ICviews* and Digitimes Asia reporting.

**Key points extracted from metadata:**
- Naura Technology Group shipped first 600mm × 600mm PLP descum tool
- Marks transition from wafer-level packaging (WLP) to panel-level packaging (PLP) equipment
- China-based equipment maker entering AI chip packaging equipment market
- Date: June 3, 2026 (article published)
- Keywords: Advanced Packaging, AI chip, China, equipment, Naura Technology, packaging, PLP

**Context:**
Naura Technology Group is one of China's leading semiconductor equipment makers, primarily known for etch and CVD tools in the front-end process. This PLP descum tool shipment signals Naura's strategic expansion into advanced packaging equipment, competing with established players like ACM Research, Applied Materials, and AMAT in the panel-level packaging equipment space.

A 600mm × 600mm descum tool targets the panel-level packaging market specifically (standard wafer is 300mm). This is consistent with the industry push toward larger panels for cost reduction (TSMC CoPoS 310×310mm, ASE 310mm automated line, USHIO 510mm glass substrates).
