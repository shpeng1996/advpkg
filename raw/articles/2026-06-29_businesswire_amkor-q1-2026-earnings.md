Amkor Technology reported record first-quarter 2026 results, driven by strong advanced packaging demand. Q1 2026 revenue reached a record $1.68 billion, up approximately 27% year-over-year. Net income was $83 million ($0.33 diluted EPS, beating consensus estimates of roughly $0.23), with EBITDA of $285 million.

Gross margin improved to 14.2% and operating margin to 6.0%, as better factory utilization more than offset a higher material mix, overhead and compensation costs.

Advanced packaging technologies — including High-Density Fan-Out (HDFO) and flip chip — were the primary drivers of the revenue beat, with record advanced packaging revenue in the quarter. Communications and premium smartphones drove notable year-over-year gains, alongside continued demand from automotive customers.

For Q2 2026, Amkor guided to continued sequential growth and margin expansion, projecting revenue between $1.75 billion and $1.85 billion with gross margin of 14.5%-15.5%.
