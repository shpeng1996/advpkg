Applied Materials has introduced six new semiconductor manufacturing systems for DRAM and advanced packaging, expanding its portfolio of 3D semiconductor equipment aimed at improving the performance and power efficiency of next-generation memory used in artificial intelligence (AI) processors.

The company unveiled the new product lineup during its 2026 DRAM & Advanced Packaging Master Class held on June 25. The portfolio includes an epitaxy system for next-generation DRAM, a chemical mechanical planarization (CMP) system for advanced packaging, an electrochemical deposition (ECD) system for 3D stacking, a plasma-enhanced chemical vapor deposition (PECVD) system for high-bandwidth memory (HBM), and two eBeam-based metrology and defect inspection systems for advanced packaging.

## Six New Systems

### 1. Centura™ Prime™ Epi (Enhanced) — DRAM Epitaxy
- Enhanced version for DRAM manufacturing
- Selectively grows doped SiGe and SiP in transistor source and drain regions
- Precise doping control; advanced strain engineering maximizing compressive stress in channel regions
- Increases drive current and transistor efficiency → faster, lower-power DRAM
- 20% smaller footprint vs. previous generation

### 2. Opta™ — CMP for Advanced Packaging
- Planarizes wafer surfaces for hybrid bonding applications
- Continuously monitors wafer conditions during polishing; dynamically adjusts process parameters in real time
- Improves within-wafer uniformity; reduces total thickness variation
- Suitable for thick films, multiple layers, complex topographies

### 3. Nokota™ VMax™ 2 — ECD for 3D Stacking
- High-precision copper plating via Adaptive Pattern Tuning (APT)
- Dynamically controls electric field during plating
- Improves copper deposition uniformity; reduces defects
- Designed for TSV filling and microbump formation in advanced packaging

### 4. Producer™ Avila™ 2 — PECVD for HBM
- Deposits dielectric films around TSV structures
- Balances mechanical stress; suppresses wafer warpage
- Critical for HBM multi-die thinning and stacking to prevent bonding failures

### 5. VeritySEM™ 7AP — eBeam CD Metrology
- Critical dimension (CD) metrology for advanced packaging
- Measures 3D features: hybrid bonding Cu pads, TSVs, microbumps
- Sub-10nm sensitivity even on warped substrates
- Detects defects smaller than 10nm

### 6. SEMVision™ G7AP — eBeam Defect Analysis
- Optical inspection-based defect review platform using high-resolution eBeam
- Identifies fine defects; auto-classifies defect types
- Accelerates yield analysis for advanced packaging
- Supports organic and glass substrates
