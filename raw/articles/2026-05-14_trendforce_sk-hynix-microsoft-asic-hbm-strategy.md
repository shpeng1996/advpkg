Microsoft is continuing to deepen its custom chip push, with SK hynix expected to play an increasingly important role. SK hynix CEO Kwak Noh-Jung is set to meet Microsoft co-founder Bill Gates and CEO Satya Nadella at the closed-door Microsoft CEO Summit 2026 in Redmond, Washington.

This signals the Korean memory chipmaker is becoming increasingly important to Microsoft's efforts to build AI infrastructure less dependent on NVIDIA.

## Key Facts

**Microsoft-SK Hynix relationship:**
- Microsoft sources DRAM and NAND flash from SK hynix
- Microsoft adopted SK hynix's **fifth-generation HBM3E** for the **Maia 200 AI accelerator**
- Maia 200 deployed at Iowa data center; expanding to Arizona facility

**Microsoft CapEx 2026:**
- Microsoft raised 2026 CapEx outlook to **US$190 billion** (TrendForce, ~130% YoY growth)
- Approximately **US$25 billion** attributed to rising component costs
- North American CSPs' CapEx expansion outpacing global average; investment concentrated on high-performance GPU clusters and in-house ASICs

**SK hynix's position:**
- Secured world's first mass-production system for HBM4 (September 2025)
- Strengthened packaging partnership with TSMC
- Built Cheongju M15X fab
- Established dedicated HBM unit, Global AI Research Center, and global production infrastructure

(Sources: The Korea Herald, JoongAng Economy News, Chosun Biz, TrendForce)
