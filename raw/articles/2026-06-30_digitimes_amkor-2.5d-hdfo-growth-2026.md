Semiconductor OSAT leader Amkor has announced plans to boost its capital expenditure budget for 2026 to US$2.5-3 billion, prioritizing expansion of advanced packaging capacities such as 2.5D and high-density fan-out (HDFO) in South Korea and Taiwan. This move aims to capitalize on the booming AI data-center CPU market. The headline indicates Amkor expects roughly triple revenue growth in its 2.5D and HDFO packaging segments for 2026 versus prior-year levels.
