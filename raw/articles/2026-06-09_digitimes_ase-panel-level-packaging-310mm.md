# ASE accelerates panel-level packaging push with fully automated 310mm line by 2026

DIGITIMES, Feb 25, 2026 (paywalled — only summary/lede accessible)

## Key claims and data points (from available summary)

- Continued AI/HPC chip demand keeps TSMC's CoWoS capacity tight into 2026, pushing major Taiwanese OSATs — ASE Holdings, SPIL, and Powertech (PTI) — to accelerate advanced-packaging expansion to capture AI-related orders.
- ASE is pursuing a fully automated 310mm (310x310mm) panel-level packaging line targeted for 2026, aligning with the "310x310mm" panel format DIGITIMES has reported as an emerging industry standard (per related coverage: "TSMC, ASE close ranks on PLP, 310x310mm emerges as the format to watch," 2025-06-26).
- Related coverage indicates ASE raised its 2026 capex to a record US$8.5 billion on strong advanced-packaging demand (DIGITIMES, 2026-04-30), and that Powertech plans to invest NT$43.3 billion to accelerate FOPLP, targeting mass production in 1H 2027 (DIGITIMES, 2026-01-28).
