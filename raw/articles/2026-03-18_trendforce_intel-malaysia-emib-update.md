## Summary

TrendForce reports (2026-03-18) on Intel's accelerating advanced packaging ramp, including Malaysia facility go-live and EMIB roadmap details.

### EMIB Roadmap Update
- EMIB expected to ramp more noticeably in **2H 2026** (second half of 2026)
- Intel showcasing **EMIB and EMIB-T roadmap** at customer events; EMIB positioned as offering capabilities beyond TSMC's CoWoS
- Target package size: **120×120 mm** — up from the industry-standard 100×100 mm used in NVIDIA Blackwell
- **EMIB-T** structure designed to support next-generation **HBM4** integration
- EMIB process established at **Amkor's Songdo K5 facility** (South Korea) — Intel's first outsourcing of high-end packaging

### Foveros Portfolio Additions
- **Foveros-R** and **Foveros-B**: two new additions to the Foveros architecture providing efficient and flexible options for customers
- Foveros 2.5D packaging technology overview published 2025-07 (Intel product brief)

### Malaysia Complex
- Intel's advanced packaging complex in **Malaysia** slated to become operational in **2026**
- Will handle die sort and prep operations
- Supports both EMIB and Foveros packaging flows
