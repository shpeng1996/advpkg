# Google TPU Push Reportedly Meets Cautious Early Adoption from AI Infrastructure Providers

**Source**: TrendForce News, 2026-05-06

While Google has signaled a strategic shift to offer its Tensor Processing Units (TPUs) to select external customers—moving beyond its legacy internal-only model—market reception among leading AI infrastructure providers remains cautious. Citing The Information, the Chosun Daily reports that specialized AI cloud powerhouses, including Nebius, Lambda, and CoreWeave, continue to anchor their hardware strategies firmly with NVIDIA.

## Google's TPU Commercialization Move

Alphabet said during its earnings call (late April/early May 2026) that it plans to sell its TPUs directly to select customers, enabling them to deploy the chips in their own data centers. The move comes shortly after Google unveiled two new TPU variants:
- **TPU 8t**: for training workloads
- **TPU 8i**: for inference workloads

## Market Reception Remains NVIDIA-Dominated

The Information cites Nebius Chief Revenue Officer Mark Boroditzky: "About 99% of customer demand is still for NVIDIA GPUs. TPU interest is rare and mostly comes from former Google users."

Key competitive deals showing NVIDIA's dominance:
- September 2025: NVIDIA signed US$1.5 billion agreement with Lambda to lease ~18,000 GPUs over 4 years
- November 2025: Lambda announced multibillion-dollar deal with Microsoft for AI infrastructure using tens of thousands of NVIDIA chips
- CoreWeave: continues to anchor strategy firmly with NVIDIA

## Advanced Packaging Implications

Google's TPU v9 was reported in earlier TrendForce coverage (2026-04-07) as evaluating Intel EMIB packaging for the 2027 generation. The cautious market reception of TPUs as standalone products suggests:
- Google may continue aggressive investment in custom packaging (Intel EMIB, TSMC CoWoS) to differentiate TPU performance
- NVIDIA's dominance in AI infrastructure reinforces CoWoS demand: every NVIDIA H100/H200/B200/R100 GPU uses CoWoS packaging
- AI ASIC ecosystem (Google TPU, Amazon Trainium, Microsoft Maia) remains a secondary but growing market for advanced packaging

## Key Data Points

- Alphabet (Google) planning TPU sales to external customers (Q2 2026 announcement)
- TPU 8t (training) and TPU 8i (inference) newly unveiled
- Market share: ~99% of cloud AI GPU demand still for NVIDIA
- Google TPU v9 (2027): evaluating Intel EMIB packaging (per earlier TrendForce reports)
- NVIDIA/Lambda deal: $1.5B, 18,000 GPUs, 4 years (Sept 2025)
