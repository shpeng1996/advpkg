# [News] SK hynix Set to Post Record Operating Margin in Q2, as HBM Mix Weighs on ASP Growth and LTAs Gain Focus

- **Q2 earnings report scheduled: 2026-07-29** — key market event as investors weigh AI semiconductor rally

## HBM Mix Effect

- HBM's high price and high margin contribution are driving operating profit to record levels
- However, HBM's high ASP mix effect slightly weights down overall average selling price growth for conventional DRAM (HBM has lower unit volume than DRAM)

## LTA Strategy Focus

- SK hynix's Long-Term Agreement (LTA) approach: previously removed price cap in LTAs (industry-unique)
- Contract terms extended to 3–5 years
- Focus shifting to LTA structure and pricing negotiations with AI hyperscalers

## Context from Prior Wiki Knowledge

- Q1 2026 operating margin was 72% (record at the time)
- SK hynix is the world's HBM leader with ~62-70% market share
- HBM4 supply in production for NVIDIA Vera Rubin platform (Q3 2026 shipments)
- HBM4E 12-layer samples shipped to key customers (2026-06-18, 48GB/16Gbps/4TB·s)
