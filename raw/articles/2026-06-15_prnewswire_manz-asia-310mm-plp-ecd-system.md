# Manz Asia Successfully Delivers World's First 310mm x 310mm Panel-Level Packaging ECD Production System

Manz Asia announced on June 15, 2026 that it has successfully delivered the world's first 310mm x 310mm panel-level packaging (PLP) Electrochemical Deposition (ECD) mass-production system, branded as part of its "Omni x-series" platform.

Key details:

- The ECD platform is designed with high flexibility to support both glass and metal square carriers, and integrates wet chemical process modules for Redistribution Layer (RDL) fabrication.
- It is engineered for FOPLP (Fan-Out Panel-Level Packaging), CoPoS (Chip-on-Panel-on-Substrate), and TGV (Through-Glass-Via)-based advanced packaging architectures.
- The 310mm x 310mm format offers advantages in scalability, panel utilization, and production yield, positioning it as enabling technology for panel-level packaging supporting AI, HPC, HBM, and high-speed interconnect applications.
- The Omni x-series spans 310mm, 510mm, and 700mm panel platforms, covering a broad range of panel-level manufacturing applications.

This is described as a world-first delivery milestone for full-scale 310mm-format PLP ECD production equipment, signaling that panel-level packaging equipment infrastructure is moving from pilot to production-capable scale in 2026.
