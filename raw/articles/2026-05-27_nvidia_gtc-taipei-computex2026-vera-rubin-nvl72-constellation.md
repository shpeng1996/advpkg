NVIDIA GTC Taipei at COMPUTEX 2026 — rolling coverage including CEO Jensen Huang's keynote (June 1, 11am Taipei time), event highlights, and live demos.

## Key Updates (in reverse chronological order)

### Wednesday May 27 — NVIDIA and Quanta Computer dinner
Jensen Huang dined with Quanta Computer founder/chairman Barry Lam and VP C.C. Leung. Huang gave a glimpse into the remainder of 2026: "The second half of this year is going to be very, very busy with Grace Blackwell, Vera Rubin, and we have a **surprise new product** that we haven't told anyone about yet."

NVIDIA's Taiwan ecosystem has grown: 10 partners years ago → ~50 partners five years ago → **150 partners** now.

### Tuesday May 26 — NVIDIA Constellation campus unveiled
Taipei Mayor Chiang Wan-an presented Jensen Huang with a key to the city at the NVIDIA Constellation event.

NVIDIA Constellation campus details:
- Location: Beitou-Shilin Technology Park, northern Taipei
- Site: ~4 hectares
- Capacity: ~4,000 employees
- Design: reflects NVIDIA Santa Clara HQ iconic design
- Function: One of the largest AI R&D hubs in APAC

### Tuesday May 26 — Jensen Huang + TSMC CEO C.C. Wei dinner
NVIDIA and TSMC CEOs dined together in Taipei on May 26. The companies "have long worked together to push the limits of what advanced semiconductor chips can make possible." The dinner underscored TSMC's role as NVIDIA's critical packaging and process partner for Vera Rubin.

### Thursday May 21 — COMPUTEX 2026 Best Choice Awards
NVIDIA won multiple COMPUTEX 2026 Best Choice Awards (BCA):

1. **NVIDIA Vera Rubin NVL72** — Golden Award + Sustainable Tech Special Award
2. **NVIDIA Jetson Thor** — Golden Award
3. **NVIDIA Alpamayo** — Vehicle Technology and Smart Cockpit Category Award

## Vera Rubin NVL72 — Packaging Details

NVIDIA Vera Rubin NVL72 rack-scale AI supercomputer:
- **Architecture**: 36 NVIDIA Vera CPUs + 72 NVIDIA Rubin GPUs
- **Scale-up fabric**: 6th-gen NVIDIA NVLink Switch
- **Scale-out/across**: ConnectX-9 SuperNICs + **Spectrum-X Ethernet Photonics co-packaged optics (CPO) switches**
- **Storage/security**: BlueField-4 DPUs
- **Memory**: HBM4 (8-layer)
- **Cooling**: 100% liquid-cooled, operates at 45°C
- **Power**: 6× onboard energy storage for intelligent power smoothing
- **Assembly**: cable-free, hose-free, fanless modular tray; 5 minutes per compute tray (vs. 2 hours previously)
- **Performance**: up to 10× higher inference performance per watt; 10× lower cost per token; with Groq 3 LPX → 35× higher throughput per watt for trillion-parameter models
- **Supply chain**: "almost 2 million parts, 150 different ecosystem partners in Taiwan"
- Jensen Huang quote: "Vera Rubin is the largest product launch, probably in the history of Taiwan."

3. **Surprise 2H26 product** teased — may require additional advanced packaging capacity
