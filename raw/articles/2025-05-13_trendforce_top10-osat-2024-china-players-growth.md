TrendForce's latest report on the semiconductor packaging and testing (OSAT) sector reveals that the global OSAT industry in 2024 faced dual challenges from accelerating technological advancements and ongoing industry consolidation. While ASE holdings and Amkor maintained leading positions, OSAT providers from China, such as JCET and HT-Tech, saw strong double-digit revenue growth—supported by government policies and domestic demand—posing a growing challenge to the existing market order.

## Global OSAT Market 2024

TrendForce reports that the combined revenue of the world's top 10 OSAT companies reached **US$41.56B in 2024, up 3% YoY**.

### Key Observations

**ASE Holdings (#1, $18.54B):** Led the ranking but faced headwinds from sluggish recovery in smartphones, consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors. In testing, faced intensified competition and rise of in-house testing.

**Amkor (#2, $6.32B, -2.8%):** Impacted by lingering inventory corrections in automotive segment and sluggish global vehicle sales. Intensified pricing pressure in China and Southeast Asia.

**JCET (#3, $5.00B, +19.3%):** Benefited from consumer electronics inventory clearing, AI PC ramp-up, and mid-range smartphone new platforms.

**Tongfu Microelectronics (#4, $3.32B, +5.6%):** Supported by comms/consumer electronics demand rebound and AMD (key customer) performance.

**Powertech Technology (#5, $2.28B, +1%):** Limited by absence of memory packaging/testing rebound and ongoing advanced packaging transition.

**HT-Tech (#6, $2.01B, +26%, highest growth):** Mass-produces low/mid-end packaging AND investing in high-end (AI, HPC, automotive, memory). Strong domestic China customer base.

**KYEC (#9, $910M, -14.5%):** Impacted by KLTech subsidiary sale. However, AI server/HPC chip testing demand and CoWoS testing demand growing.

### Market Structural Observations

- **China OSAT rise:** JCET (+19.3%), HT-Tech (+26%), WiseRoad (+5%), Hana Micron (+23.7%) all outperformed global average — supported by government policies and domestic semiconductor demand.
- **Industry restructuring:** From traditional manufacturing to strategic sector centered on advanced integration and R&D.
- **Technical requirements intensifying:** Heterogeneous integration, WLP, die stacking, advanced test equipment, high-frequency/high-density packaging for AI and edge computing.
- **Dual dynamic:** Stable leadership from mature players (ASE, Amkor) + rapid rise of regional challengers (Chinese OSATs).

> "The global OSAT market in 2024 is defined by a dual dynamic: stable leadership from mature players and the rapid rise of regional challengers. This trend sets the stage for intensified competition in next-generation technologies such as advanced packaging and heterogeneous integration." — TrendForce
