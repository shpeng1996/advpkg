# AI Expansion - Supply Chain Analysis For CoWoS And HBM

SemiAnalysis explains CoWoS-S as the main production flow for co-packaging AI logic and HBM on a silicon interposer, then onto an organic package substrate.

## Publicly Accessible Extracted Points

- CoWoS is a 2.5D technology where active logic and HBM dies sit on a passive silicon interposer.
- The silicon interposer has metal routing but no transistors; describing it as a "65 nm process" is imprecise.
- Silicon interposer fabrication begins with TSV formation, followed by RDL layers and copper-pillar/UBM formation.
- Known-good logic and HBM dies are attached to the interposer wafer using flip-chip mass reflow, followed by underfill and molding.
- The molded interposer is flipped and thinned to reveal TSVs; then backside bumps connect it to the package substrate.
- The public preview identifies 28 upstream vendors in the CoWoS/HBM flow, but detailed vendor analysis is paywalled.
