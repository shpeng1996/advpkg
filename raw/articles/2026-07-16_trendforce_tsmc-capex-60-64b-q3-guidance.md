After posting a new high net profit in Q2 2026, TSMC management projects Q3 2026 revenue between $44.6 billion and $45.8 billion, representing ~37% YoY growth and ~12% sequential gain.

Profitability outlook (assumed exchange rate: 1 USD = 32 TWD):
- Q3 gross margin: 65%–67% (expected to decline due to 2nm ramp-up and overseas fab expansion)
- Q3 operating margin: 56%–58%

## CapEx Guidance Raised
TSMC aggressively lifted its 2026 capital expenditure guidance to US$60–64 billion, a significant bump from the prior US$52–56 billion forecast. This budget hike underscores the company's focus on scaling capacity for 5G, AI, and HPC.

## Revenue Outlook
TSMC raised full-year revenue growth guidance to over 40% in USD terms, up significantly from the previous 30% projection.

## Margin Headwinds
- 2nm ramp-up: expected to cause 3%–4% gross margin dilution
- Overseas fab gross margin dilution: 2%–3% in early stages, widening to 3%–4% as operations scale

(Source: TSMC)
